These are my works as Designer, and later, Assistant Lead Design (from Volume 4 onwards) at The Slow Press — an independent zine interested in looking at stories surrounding food, by people passionate about food. On top of designing spreads for the zine, as Assistant Lead Design, I also participate in the cover shoots for the zine, from conceptualization to art direction, together with the rest of our talented team.
