Welcome

Welcome to my toybox, home to pixel adopts/adoptables from the old web (and new)! While these were common in websites I visited when I was younger, I've never had one of my own until now. I've tried to salvage the older pixels from archive.org to give them a new home here! Clicking on the pixel should link back to where they're from. All these external links open in a new tab.

You may navigate using the buttons below, or if there are accessibility issues, the adoptables are sorted in the following categories:

  • Characters and animals
  • Edibles
  • Miscellenous/Others
