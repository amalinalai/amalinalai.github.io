Welcome

Welcome to lina's toybox, home to adoptables from the old web (and new)! While these were common in websites I visited when I was younger, I've never had one of my own until now. I've tried to salvage the older pixels from archive.org to give them a new home here! Clicking on the pixel should link back to where they're from. All these external links open in a new tab.

Currently, none of the adoptables have alt-text descriptions, I'm sorry! I will get to them when I can.

Do note that there will be image heavy (small pixels and gifs) pages!
