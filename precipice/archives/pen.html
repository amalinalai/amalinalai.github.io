pen collection

aka the first "shrine"

I like fountain (& dip) pens, and I wanted to create a special page to hold information on all the pens I have in my collection! I try to not lean into the excessive overconsumption that can come with this hobby, and choose to buy only pens that feel different enough from the rest of my other pens to justify its purchase.

version 1.0 › feb 2024 — ???

I thought about how cute it would be to feature not only images of my pen, but the writing that comes out from it, particularly with the different inks that come with the hobby as well. I think I was partly inspired by the are.na channel "I miss knowing what everyone's writing looks like".

The layout itself is simple pretty simple; it consists of all my pens arranged vertically on the page. On hover, the pen changes (e.g. uncaps, or is turned in a different direction) to feature another angle or perspective of the pen. On clicking, an image of a flashcard with handwritten information corresponding to that particular card will pop up. The cards can be dragged around or closed when done. Unfortunately, the only solutions to implement this feature required javascript. As I detail on the page, I had a lot of help from this solution for open/close div toggles and this solution for multiple draggable divs to implement the draggable cards.

I also made use of the details element to add a transcript for each card in their corresponding div that can be overlayed when toggled on. This transcript can be toggled on and off thanks to this. Honestly, I was pretty proud of being able to implement this transcript feature with just the details element!

At the moment, there is no mobile-friendly version yet. I am planning to get on it!
