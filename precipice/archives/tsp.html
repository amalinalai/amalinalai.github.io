the slow press portfolio

aka chronicling my designs!

As I added more information about myself to my professional page, I wanted to add a page to showcase all the spreads I've designed for The Slow Press. Art has always been a part of my life, and I wanted some place to safekeep the things I was doing outside of my research and science.

version 1.0 › march 2023 — forever, probably

Frankly, the webpage is was designed to be purely functional, consisting of an outer div which houses my text and a table showcasing my works. I didn't bother with making it a responsive design, and I don't think I have any intention to. Pretty sure I also referenced this stackoverflow question to highlight a table row on hover.
