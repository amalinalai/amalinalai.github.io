what is this?

Welcome to my map of the internet, built with Tiana Dueck's guide. In a way, this map acts a means to navigate the internet in a much more intentional, personal and meaningful manner.

The planets in the map above are clickable and will lead to different pages that serve as a waypoint to places on the internet. This version was built off the idea of planets in orbit. The source sits in the middle, and the planets, or waypoints, orbit around this sun. I intend to add paths from planet to planet, but have not done so!

The pixel art is all mine, while the responsive image map was achieved through this responsive image map generator together with this solution.
