what is this?

Welcome to my map of the internet, built with Tiana Dueck's guide. In a way, this map acts a means to navigate the internet in a much more intentional, personal and meaningful manner.

The buildings in the map above are clickable and will lead to different pages that serve as a waypoint to places on the internet that are valueable, insightful or interesting to me. This version was built off the idea of Chinese art in vertical scrolls, whereby different imagery would slowly reveal itself top down as the scroll is read.

The pixel art is all mine, while the responsive image map was achieved through this responsive image map generator together with this solution.
