what is this?

Welcome to my map of the internet, built with Tiana Dueck's guide. In a way, this map acts a means to navigate the internet in a much more intentional, personal and meaningful manner. I like to think of it as a mix between a guidebook and a curated bookmarks bar — when I'm lost on the internet, I hope to come back to this page to find my footing in how I wish to explore.

The planets in the map above are clickable and will lead to different pages that serve as a waypoint to places on the internet. This version was built off the idea of planets in orbit. The source sits in the middle, and the planets, or waypoints, orbit around this sun.

The pixel art is all mine, while the responsive image map was achieved through this responsive image map generator together with this solution.
