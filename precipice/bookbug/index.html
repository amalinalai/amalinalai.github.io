what this page?

Welcome to lina's bookbug page! Here you'll find book reviews of books I've read as part of the bookbug club. If you'd like to read my opinions on other books, you can also check out my bookshelf. The page styling is still under construction!

what is bookbug?

From the bookbug website:

Bookbug is a digital book club that is centered around discussions and reviews. Each month members will read a book and publish their thoughts to their personal sites.
