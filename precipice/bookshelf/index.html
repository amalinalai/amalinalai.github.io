28 Apr The Inheritance Games Jennifer Lynn Barnes
No! more! love! triangles! The book was definitely a page turner, but the romance subplot (a love triangle, no less) left me rolling my eyes a fair bit. Some puzzles demanded a little too much suspension of disbelief from me. And I was not a huge fan of the ending. It felt like I'd read that 500 pages only for it to not answer half the questions for the next book in the trilogy.
12 May The Searcher Tana French
A very, very slow book that I almost abandoned but stayed for the characters. French has a very beautiful way of writing too, but the pacing left me at times tempted to skim through it to reach some turn in the plot. I think it got better along the way and I found myself enjoying the last half of the book.
18 May Murder on The Orient Express Agatha Christie
