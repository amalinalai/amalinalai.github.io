Optimal Vaccination Roll-out for COVID-19 Vaccines to Limit Spread & Deaths
April 2021 — May 2021
Computational Physics Module Final Project
Nanyang Technological University, Singapore

  • Jupyter
  • Python
  • Numerical
  • Modified the existing Susceptible Exposed Infectious Recovered (SEIR) model to account for type of vaccination rollout, exclude natural births and deaths, and include the differing effects of the virus on different age groups.
  • Solved the modified differential equations of the models using the Runge-Kutta 4 method in Python to visualize the infection rates with time for vaccinated and unvaccinated communities.
  • Optimized the vaccination strategy across age groups in the modified SEIR model to minimize deaths due to COVID using the Nelder-Mead algorithm implemented in Python, assuming a limited amount of vaccines available.
