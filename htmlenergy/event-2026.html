when and where?

15 August 2026, 2pm-5pm @ Bukit Panjang Public Library. (map)

RSVP not essential, but appreciated:

Email is not required but providing it will allow me to send you a reminder and any updates regarding the event!

what is this?

This is a HTML freewrite, an event where people get together to (learn and) handwrite HTML documents as part of HTML Day!

HTML documents form the backbone of every website you've ever visited. Before website builders, people made websites by coding their own HTML documents.

who can come?

Literally anyone — no coding skills required! Everyone can learn to code in HTML. Most importantly, we'll help each other out.

what should I bring?

Essential: something to write HTML on (e.g., fully charged laptop [recommended], mobile phone, pen & paper)

Optional: a friend, and some ideas for HTML page(s)

who is hosting this?

Lina and Trevor, in collaboration with Dilip from NLB! If you need to contact us, you can reach us at htmlinsg [at] pm.me

I'm not in Singapore. Is there an event in my city?

Check out html.energy/events.html. If your city isn't listed, feel free to organize your own!
