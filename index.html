Amalina Lai [she/her]

I am a new PhD student and recipient of the Nanyang President's Graduate Scholarship at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, under the supervision of Mile Gu. Currently, my research interests lie in continuous variable systems and (virtual) distillation, but I am also keen to learn more about error-correction.

Outside of physics, I am interested in pop-culture analysis, brutalist web design, and the ways in which information from seemingly disparate fields interconnect to provide us with a toolset to encounter the world. When I'm not working on science, I am part of the The Slow Press — an independent zine interested in looking at stories surrounding food — as their Assistant Lead Design.

I also enjoy looking at art, drawing and (amateur) birding in my free time.
