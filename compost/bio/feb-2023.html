February 2023

I am a new PhD student and recipient of the Nanyang President's Graduate Scholarship at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, under the supervision of Mile Gu. Currently, my research interests lie in continuous variable systems and (virtual) distillation, but I am also keen to learn more about error-correction.

Outside of physics, I am interested in pop-culture analysis, brutalist web design, and the ways in which information from seemingly disparate fields interconnect to provide us with a toolset to encounter the world. I believe websites can be more than just professional, which is why this one houses a personal side. I also enjoy looking at art, drawing and (amateur) birding in my free time.
